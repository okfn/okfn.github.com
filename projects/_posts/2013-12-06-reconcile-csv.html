--- layout: project title: reconcile-csv projecturl: http://okfnlabs.org/reconcile-csv imageurl: http://okfnlabs.org/reconcile-csv/img/matching.png tags: [] author: Michael Bauer maintainers: [mihi] github_user: okfn github_repo: reconcile-csv permalink: /projects/reconcile-csv/index.html slug: reconcile-csv language: [clojure] type: [webapp] stage: live --- Reconcile-CSV is an OpenRefine reconciliation service running on top of a CSV file. It uses fuzzy matching to find the most likely candidates for matching. If you ever needed to join two datasets that didn't have unique identifiers and where things are written slightly different: this is a way to go.
